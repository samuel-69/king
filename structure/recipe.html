simple omelette recipe

An easy and quick dish,perfect for any meal. this classic omelette combines beaten eggscooked to perfection optionally filled with your choice of cheese,vegetables or meats.

Preparation time

  • total: Approximately 10 minutes
  • Preparation: 5 minutes
  • cooking: 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • salt,to taste
  • pepper,to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of butter or oil
  • optional fillings:cheese,diced vegetables,cockedmeats,herbs
  • home

Instructions

  1. beat the eggs: in a bowl beat the eggs eith a pinch of salt and pepperuntil they are all well mixed you can add the tablespoon of water or milk for a fluffer of texture.
  2. Heat the pan: place a non stick fring pan over medium heat and add a butter oil.
  3. cook the omelette: once the butter is melted and bubbling,pour the eggs tilt the pan to ensure the eggs evenly coat the surface.
  4. Add fillings (optional): when the eggs begin to set at the edges but are still slightly runny in the middle ,sprinkle your choosen feelings over one half of the omellete.
  5. Fold and serve: as the omelette continues to cook,carefully lift one edge and fold it over the fillings.lets cook another minute,then slide onto a plate.
  6. Enjoy: serve hot,with additional salt and pepper if needed.
